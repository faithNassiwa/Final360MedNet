Health care professionals are constantly faced with the challenge of keeping updated with latest research and best practices in medicine. With varying backgrounds and resources, there’s visible inequity in accessing credible information as well as building collaborative networks with other professionals. We, however, believe that the healthcare workforce, with dissimilar experiences and differing approaches can harness synergies if connected.

Platforms that bring together people who think differently; have different skills; have access to varying resources or use different approaches but are able to network minds, can foster innovative solutions.

360MedNet seeks to leverage the massive mobile and internet connectivity among health care professionals in Africa to harness partnerships. We believe that exploration of such a global networking space will create vital solutions through sharing of diverse ideas and opinions.

The instant and timely connection provides a fertile avenue for connections, collaborations and consequently, creation of solutions that impact individual patient outcomes and professional performance in real time.



Connect

Do you have a very tight work schedule? Is your academic program depriving you of diverse mentorship? Would you like to be in touch with other health professionals of similar interests?

360MedNet brings together students and medical professionals from the fields of Pharmacy, Nursing, Medicine,Lab Technology, Radiology etc. These also bring various interests in several areas like Infectious diseases,Drugs and therapeutics, Microbiology, Nursing practice and Cardiology among others.

Collaborate

Collaborate to solve challenges through integration of diverse proficiencies.

Do not let the quality of your medical service be limited to your core field of training. Expand your knowledge and skills through tapping into the wide variety of medical knowledge possessed by others on 360MedNet. Embrace the richness and depth of information drawn from other people’s backgrounds to enhance your output. 360MedNet expands your immediate horizon through helping you collaborate beyond the confines of your geographical location.

Create

Create innovative solutions in Health care.

Diversity breaks stereotypes. Diversity opens up and enhances educational experiences. Diversity helps people appreciate new notions.

Through shared knowledge, experiences, backgrounds and worldviews, 360MedNet is creating a hub for innovation in health care.




Vision

An intimately connected and enlightened healthcare workforce.

Mission

Building online platforms that break barriers to peer and mentorship support through open opinion sharing.
